What is Monkey Fi?

Monkey Fi is a powerful and modern news application that provides you with the latest updates from around the world. Whether it's breaking news, trending topics, or local updates, Monkey Fi brings it all to your fingertips.

Our Mission

Our goal is to keep you informed with accurate, real-time news while offering a clean and user-friendly experience. We believe information should be accessible, beautiful, and smart.

Features

  • Live News Updates
  • Categories like Sports, Tech, Entertainment, and World News
  • Dark/Light Mode Toggle (coming soon)
  • Minimal ads and fast loading

Meet the Team

Monkey Fi is built by a small team of passionate developers and designers who care about delivering quality content and seamless design.
